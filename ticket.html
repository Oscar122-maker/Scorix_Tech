Support guide

How priority is handled.

Critical

Systems are down, work is stopped, or there is a serious security concern.

High

A major workflow is affected but there may be a temporary workaround.

Medium

Some users or services are affected, but business can continue.

Low

General requests, small changes, planned maintenance, or advice.
